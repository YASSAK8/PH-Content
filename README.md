## ▶️ How to Use PH-Content

Once installed, open the app and follow these steps:

1. **Copy the video link**  
   Go to PornHub or any supported site in your browser. Find the video you want to save. Copy the URL from your browser’s address bar.

2. **Paste the link in PH-Content**  
   Go back to the PH-Content window. You will see a box labeled "Video URL". Paste the copied link there.

3. **Choose video quality**  
   PH-Content will fetch a list of available video qualities. Pick the one you prefer (higher quality files take longer to download).

4. **Select download location**  
   Pick a folder where you want the video saved. By default, it will use your "Videos" folder in Windows.

5. **Start download**  
   Click the "Download" button. The progress bar will show the download status. Larger videos might take a few minutes depending on your internet speed.

6. **Watch your video**  
   When the download finishes, open the folder to find your video. You can watch it with any video player that supports `.mp4`.

If you face any issues, try the following:

- Disable antivirus temporarily if it blocks the app (remember to turn it back on).
